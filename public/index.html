My most basic studytime unit is t = 10mins, henceforth all my study and practice activites are divided up in units of 10mins.

I categorieze my incoming infromation into 3 categories: A. Can, 12 problems, total 12t(2hrs) B. ZPD, 4 problems, total 12t(2hrs) C. Can't. 1 problem, total 12t(2hrs)

I start my day trying to attack category C.
